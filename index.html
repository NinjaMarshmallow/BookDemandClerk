Book Demand Clerk App

Senior Project of Josh Bussis and Jason Klaassen for Calvin University

This web application found here.

Purpose

The purpose of this application is to automate an interaction between the faculty and the Hekman Library.

Goals

The first goal is to give a hospitable user interface for the faculty to quickly click through to secure books they would like to order for the library's collection.

The second goal is to automate the department chair's responsibilities of collecting and sending out the votes via email by utilizing a database.
